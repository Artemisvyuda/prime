HOME

Welfare Checks: We prioritize the safety and well-being of our clients. Through regular visits, we monitor their condition, offer support, and ensure they feel secure in their homes. Light Housework & Laundry: We understand that everyday chores like cleaning and laundry can become overwhelming. Our compassionate team takes care of these tasks, allowing clients to focus on what matters most – spending time with loved ones, pursuing hobbies, or simply relaxing. Companionship: Social interaction and emotional support are crucial for overall well-being. We provide companionship services to combat loneliness and isolation, fostering meaningful connections with our clients. Shopping Errands & Mail Management: We assist with essential tasks like grocery shopping and managing mail. This ensures that daily needs are met and that important documents are handled efficiently. Form Completion & Transportation Booking: Navigating paperwork and arranging transportation can be stressful. Our team offers support by helping clients complete forms and booking reliable transportation options, easing everyday burdens and promoting independence.

SUPPORT

Sitting service: This service provides companionship for your loved one or respite for the caregiver, allowing them to take a break. It requires a minimum booking of 4 hours. Sleep-in support: A support worker stays with the individual or their loved one at night for reassurance. The worker needs a place to rest and requires a minimum booking of 8 hours. Overnight support (waking night): The support worker stays awake throughout the night, providing assistance as needed. This service also requires a minimum booking of 8 hours. These services aim to ensure the safety, well-being, and comfort of the individual, while also providing respite and support for their caregivers.
